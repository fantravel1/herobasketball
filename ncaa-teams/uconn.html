The UConn Huskies are the most successful program in women's basketball history, with 11 NCAA Championships under legendary coach Geno Auriemma. The program set records for excellence including an unprecedented 111-game winning streak (2014-2017) and four consecutive national titles (2013-2016).

UConn has produced more WNBA All-Stars and Olympic gold medalists than any other program. From Rebecca Lobo to Diana Taurasi to Breanna Stewart, the Huskies have defined women's basketball excellence for over three decades.
