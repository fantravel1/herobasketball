The South Carolina Gamecocks have become the premier program in women's college basketball under Dawn Staley. The program has won three national championships (2017, 2022, 2024) and completed a perfect 38-0 season in 2024.

Staley arrived in Columbia in 2008 and transformed the Gamecocks from an SEC afterthought into the nation's dominant program. The 2024 championship team is considered one of the greatest college basketball teams ever assembled, going wire-to-wire as the #1 ranked team.

South Carolina has become a destination for elite recruits, consistently landing top-five recruiting classes and developing players for the WNBA, including A'ja Wilson, Aliyah Boston, and many more.
