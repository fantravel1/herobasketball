The Tennessee Lady Vols are the most decorated program in women's basketball history, with 8 NCAA championships under legendary coach Pat Summitt (1952-2016). Every player who completed eligibility under Summitt graduated. The court is named "The Summitt" in her honor.

Summitt's 1,098 career wins stood as the all-time record until 2024. The program has produced countless WNBA stars and Olympians, creating the template for elite women's basketball programs.
