The Notre Dame Fighting Irish have been a consistent powerhouse, winning championships in 2001 and 2018 under legendary coach Muffet McGraw. Current coach Niele Ivey won a national title as a player at Notre Dame in 2001 and returned to lead her alma mater in 2020.

The 2018 championship team featured Arike Ogunbowale's iconic buzzer-beaters against UConn and Mississippi State. Notre Dame has reached 9 Final Fours, establishing itself as an elite program.
