Ticha Penicheiro

Sacramento Monarchs (Retired)Point GuardPortugal5'11"
WNBA Champion 2005 4x Assist Champion Portuguese Pioneer

Ticha Penicheiro was one of the most creative passers in WNBA history. A four-time assist champion, she could find teammates in ways that seemed impossible.

The Portuguese point guard led Sacramento to the 2005 WNBA title and finished her career as one of the greatest playmakers the league has ever seen. Her no-look passes became legendary.
