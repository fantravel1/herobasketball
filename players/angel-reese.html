üìñ Biography

Angel Reese has taken the WNBA by storm in her rookie season, becoming one of the most dominant rebounders the league has ever seen. Her physical style of play and magnetic personality have made her must-watch television.

üëë Known as the "Bayou Barbie", Angel embraces her dual identity as a fierce competitor on the court and a fashion icon off it. Her confidence and marketability have made her one of the most popular rookies in WNBA history.

Born on May 6, 2002, in Randallstown, Maryland, Reese played college basketball at Maryland before transferring to LSU, where she led the Tigers to their first-ever NCAA Championship in 2023 under coach Kim Mulkey.

Selected 7th overall by the Chicago Sky in the 2024 WNBA Draft, Reese immediately made an impact, earning All-Star honors in her rookie season and breaking the WNBA record for consecutive double-doubles.

Her battles with Caitlin Clark, dating back to their NCAA rivalry, have helped drive unprecedented interest in women's basketball, with their matchups consistently drawing record TV ratings.
