üìñ Biography

DeWanna Bonner is a two-time WNBA champion and one of the most accomplished forwards in league history. With a career spanning 16 seasons, Bonner has established herself as a versatile scorer, reliable defender, and proven winner at the highest level.

Born on August 21, 1987, in Fairfield, Alabama, Bonner starred at Auburn University where she was a two-time SEC Player of the Year. Her combination of size, skill, and basketball IQ made her a top prospect in the 2009 WNBA Draft.

Selected 5th overall by the Phoenix Mercury, Bonner won two WNBA championships (2009, 2014) and earned five All-Star selections during her 11 seasons in Phoenix. After joining the Connecticut Sun in 2020, she has continued to be a vital contributor, averaging 15.3 points and 6.2 rebounds per game in 2024 while providing veteran leadership to a championship-contending team.
