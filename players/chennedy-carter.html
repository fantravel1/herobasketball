üìñ Biography

Chennedy Carter is one of the WNBA's most explosive and dynamic scorers. Known for her lightning-quick first step, creative shot-making, and fearless attacking style, Carter brings an exciting offensive spark to the Chicago Sky.

Born on November 14, 1998, in Mansfield, Texas, Carter became a star at Texas A&M University. In her freshman season, she won SEC Freshman of the Year honors and was named to the All-SEC First Team. She continued her dominance, earning All-SEC First Team honors again as a sophomore before declaring for the WNBA Draft.

Selected fourth overall by the Atlanta Dream in the 2020 WNBA Draft, Carter immediately showcased her scoring ability, earning All-Rookie Team honors. Her ability to create her own shot, finish through contact, and score from all three levels made her a valuable offensive weapon.

After joining the Los Angeles Sparks and then the Chicago Sky in 2024, Carter has continued to evolve her game, bringing veteran scoring punch to a Sky team looking to return to championship contention. Her aggressive mentality and scoring prowess make her one of the league's most entertaining players to watch.
