Kiki Rice is one of the premier point guards in college basketball and a projected top pick in an upcoming WNBA Draft. The UCLA star has established herself as one of the best floor generals in the country.

A McDonald's All-American and top recruit out of Washington D.C., Rice chose UCLA and immediately became the Bruins' leader. Her exceptional court vision, scoring ability, and leadership have made her one of the most complete point guards in the college game.

Rice has earned All-Pac-12 honors and represented Team USA in youth international competitions. With her elite passing ability and competitive drive, she projects as a future WNBA star and potential franchise cornerstone.
