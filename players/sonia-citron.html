Sonia Citron is a versatile guard for the Notre Dame Fighting Irish and one of the most complete players in college basketball. Her combination of scoring, playmaking, and defense makes her a cornerstone of Notre Dame's program.

A McDonald's All-American from New York, Citron chose Notre Dame and immediately made an impact. She has earned All-ACC honors and helped lead the Irish to NCAA Tournament success. Her ability to impact the game in multiple ways makes her one of the most valuable players in the country.

Citron's size, skill, and basketball IQ project her as a future WNBA player. Playing alongside Hannah Hidalgo at Notre Dame, she has helped form one of the nation's most exciting backcourts and continues to elevate her game each season.
