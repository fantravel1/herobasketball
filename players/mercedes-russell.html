Mercedes Russell

WNBACenterUSA6'5"
Tennessee Lady Vols Skilled Post Interior Presence

Mercedes Russell is a skilled center who developed her game at Tennessee under the legendary Lady Vols program. Her size and touch around the basket make her a valuable interior option.

At 6'5", Russell provides a true post presence. Her ability to score in the paint and rebound makes her a reliable contributor for any team needing interior depth.
