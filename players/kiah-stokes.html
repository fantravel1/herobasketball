üìñ Biography

Kiah Stokes is a 2024 WNBA champion and elite defensive anchor for the New York Liberty. Known for her shot-blocking prowess, rebounding ability, and defensive intensity, Stokes has established herself as one of the league's premier interior defenders.

Born on August 30, 1993, in Marion, Iowa, Stokes was a cornerstone of the UConn dynasty, winning four consecutive NCAA championships (2013-2016) under coach Geno Auriemma. Her defensive excellence and championship pedigree made her a highly valued prospect.

Selected 11th overall by the New York Liberty in the 2015 WNBA Draft, Stokes spent time both in the WNBA and playing internationally. She returned to the Liberty in 2023 and played a crucial role in their 2024 championship run, providing veteran leadership, rim protection, and rebounding while anchoring one of the league's top defenses.
