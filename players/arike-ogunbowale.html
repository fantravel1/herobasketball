Arike Ogunbowale is one of the most electrifying scorers in the WNBA and the face of the Dallas Wings franchise. Her fearless shot-making and killer instinct have made her appointment television since entering the league.

üèÄ March Madness Legend: Before the WNBA, Arike became a college basketball icon by hitting back-to-back game-winners in the 2018 Final Four and National Championship Game to give Notre Dame the title. Her NCAA Tournament heroics made her a household name.

Drafted 5th overall in 2019, Ogunbowale immediately became Dallas's go-to scorer, earning All-Star selections in each of her first four seasons. She led the league in scoring in 2020 with 22.8 PPG.

The 2021 All-Star Game MVP, Arike put on a show with 26 points, including the game-winning shot. Her confidence and swagger have made her one of the most marketable players in the league.

While still chasing a championship in Dallas, Ogunbowale continues to put up impressive numbers and remains one of the most exciting players to watch every night.
