Nyara Sabally is a talented German forward for the New York Liberty and the younger sister of Dallas Wings star Satou Sabally. The Sabally sisters have become one of the most exciting sibling duos in professional basketball.

Nyara played college basketball at Oregon, following in her sister's footsteps. She was selected 5th overall in the 2022 WNBA Draft by the New York Liberty. Despite battling injuries early in her career, she has shown flashes of her immense potential as a versatile forward.

Representing Germany internationally, Nyara brings size, skill, and basketball IQ to the Liberty. Her development alongside stars like Sabrina Ionescu and Breanna Stewart positions her for a bright future in the league.
