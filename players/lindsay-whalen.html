Lindsay Whalen

Minnesota Lynx (Retired)Point GuardUSA5'9"
4x WNBA Champion Hall of Fame 5x All-Star

Lindsay Whalen is a Minnesota basketball legend and four-time WNBA champion. She led the Lynx dynasty that won four titles in seven years, cementing her status as one of the greatest winners in league history.

A Hall of Famer and Olympic gold medalist, Whalen is now the head coach at the University of Minnesota. Her court vision, leadership, and clutch performances defined her legendary career.
