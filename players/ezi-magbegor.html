Ezi Magbegor is an emerging star for the Seattle Storm and one of Australia's brightest basketball talents. The athletic center was selected 12th overall in the 2019 WNBA Draft and has quickly developed into a key player for Seattle.

Magbegor won back-to-back WNBA championships with the Storm in 2018 and 2020, contributing significant minutes as a young player. Her development accelerated rapidly, earning her first WNBA All-Star selection in 2024 as she became Seattle's primary interior presence.

Born in Melbourne, Australia, Magbegor has represented the Australian Opals in international competition, including the Olympics. Her combination of athleticism, shot-blocking, and offensive skill around the rim makes her one of the league's most promising young centers.
