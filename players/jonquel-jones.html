Jonquel Jones is the versatile big who brought the New York Liberty their first WNBA Championship in 2024. Her ability to stretch the floor, protect the rim, and dominate the glass makes her one of the most unique players in the league.

Born in the Bahamas, Jones is one of the premier international players in WNBA history. She won the 2021 MVP with the Connecticut Sun before joining the Liberty superteam in 2023.

In the 2024 Finals, Jones earned Finals MVP honors, averaging 17.8 PPG while providing crucial defense and rebounding against the Minnesota Lynx. Her championship completed a remarkable journey from small Caribbean nation to WNBA glory.

At 6'6", Jones can play four positions and is equally comfortable posting up, shooting threes, or blocking shots. She represents the evolution of the modern WNBA big.
