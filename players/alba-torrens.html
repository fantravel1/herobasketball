Alba Torrens is one of the greatest players in Spanish basketball history and a EuroLeague legend. The versatile forward/center from Mallorca has dominated European basketball for over a decade, winning multiple championships and becoming the face of Spanish women's basketball.

Born on August 30, 1989, in Binissalem, Mallorca, Torrens' basketball journey took her to the University of Connecticut, where she played one season with the legendary Huskies. After returning to Europe, she quickly established herself as one of the continent's premier talents, combining size, skill, and basketball IQ.

Torrens has been the cornerstone of Perfumer√≠as Avenida's dynasty, leading the Salamanca-based club to three EuroLeague titles (2011, 2019, 2021) and numerous Spanish championships. Her ability to score from all three levels, facilitate for teammates, and dominate the boards has made her one of Europe's most complete players.

On the international stage, Torrens has been instrumental in Spain's success, winning Olympic silver medals and EuroBasket gold. She's the all-time leading scorer for the Spanish national team and has represented her country in four Olympic Games. Her longevity, consistency, and leadership have made her an icon of European basketball and a role model for Spanish athletes.
