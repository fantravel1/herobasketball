Monique Billings

Rebounding Machine UCLA Fan Favorite

Monique Billings is a versatile forward known for her relentless energy and rebounding prowess. As a cornerstone of the Atlanta Dream, she brings effort and intensity every night.

A UCLA product, Billings has established herself as one of the league's most reliable forwards. Her ability to crash the boards and defend multiple positions makes her a valuable two-way player.
