Allie Quigley

Chicago Sky (Retired)GuardUSA5'10"
WNBA Champion 2021 3x 3PT Champion All-Time Shooter

Allie Quigley retired as one of the greatest shooters in WNBA history. Her three consecutive Three-Point Contest championships (2017-2019, 2021) cemented her legacy as a shooting legend.

The 2021 WNBA championship with Chicago Sky crowned a remarkable career. Married to former teammate Courtney Vandersloot, Quigley's basketball IQ and clutch shooting made her a beloved figure in the sport.
