üìñ Biography

Courtney Vandersloot is the WNBA's all-time assists leader and a 2024 WNBA champion with the New York Liberty. Widely regarded as one of the greatest pure point guards in basketball history, Vandersloot has revolutionized the position with her exceptional court vision, playmaking ability, and basketball IQ.

Born on February 8, 1989, in Kent, Washington, Vandersloot starred at Gonzaga University where she became the West Coast Conference's all-time leading scorer. Her ability to orchestrate an offense and make everyone around her better made her a top prospect in the 2011 WNBA Draft.

Selected 3rd overall by the Chicago Sky, Vandersloot spent 12 seasons building a legendary career, becoming the franchise's all-time assists leader and winning the assists title twice. In 2023, she joined the New York Liberty and immediately helped transform the team into champions, averaging 7.8 points and 6.7 assists in 2024 while providing veteran leadership and the perfect floor general for a title-winning squad.
