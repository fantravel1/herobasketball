Azura Stevens is a versatile forward/center who won a WNBA championship with the Chicago Sky in 2021. Her combination of size, skill, and basketball IQ makes her a valuable contributor on both ends of the floor.

Stevens played college basketball at Duke and UConn, transferring after her sophomore year to join Geno Auriemma's program. She was drafted 6th overall by Dallas in 2018 and has since played for the Sparks, Sky, and now the Los Angeles Sparks again.

At 6'6", Stevens brings rare size and versatility to the forward position. She can stretch the floor with her shooting while also protecting the rim and rebounding. Her championship experience with Chicago in 2021 demonstrated her ability to contribute on a winning team.
