Yolanda Griffith

Sacramento Monarchs (Retired)CenterUSA6'4"
WNBA Champion 2005 MVP 1999 2x Olympic Gold

Yolanda Griffith was one of the most dominant post players in WNBA history. The 1999 MVP won both Rookie of the Year and MVP in the same season, leading the league in scoring and rebounding.

A WNBA champion with Sacramento in 2005 and two-time Olympic gold medalist, Griffith's combination of power and skill made her nearly unstoppable in the paint.
