Biography

Aliyah Boston is one of the most accomplished college basketball players in history, who transitioned seamlessly to the WNBA. Selected #1 overall by the Indiana Fever in 2023, she became one of the cornerstones of the franchise's rebuilding efforts.

At South Carolina, Boston was a dominant force, winning the 2022 NCAA Championship and being named the tournament's Most Outstanding Player. She won the Naismith Player of the Year award in 2022 and was a three-time All-American.

In her rookie WNBA season (2023), Boston won the Rookie of the Year award and was named an All-Star. In 2024, she continued to develop alongside teammate Caitlin Clark, forming one of the most exciting young duos in the league.
