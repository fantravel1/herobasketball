Allisha Gray is a versatile two-way guard who has established herself as one of the most well-rounded players in the WNBA. She won the 2023 WNBA Skills Challenge, showcasing her complete skill set.

Gray won an NCAA championship at South Carolina in 2017 under Dawn Staley before being drafted 4th overall by Dallas. After several seasons with the Wings, she joined the Atlanta Dream, where she has continued to excel as a consistent scorer and defender.

Her combination of scoring ability, defensive versatility, and basketball IQ makes her a valuable player on both ends of the floor. Gray continues to be one of the most reliable performers in the league.
