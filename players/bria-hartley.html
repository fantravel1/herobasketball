Bria Hartley

WNBAGuardUSA5'9"
2x NCAA Champion UConn Dynamic Scorer

Bria Hartley is a two-time NCAA champion from UConn who has brought her winning pedigree to the WNBA. Her ability to score in bunches makes her a valuable offensive weapon.

Known for her quickness and ability to get to the basket, Hartley has overcome injuries to continue her professional career. Her championship experience and competitive fire remain elite.
