Natasha Howard

Dallas WingsForwardUSA6'2"
2x WNBA Champion DPOY 2019 All-Star

Natasha Howard is a two-time WNBA champion and former Defensive Player of the Year. Her versatility on both ends of the floor makes her one of the most complete forwards in the league.

Howard won championships with Minnesota and Seattle, showcasing her ability to contribute to winning teams. Her combination of size, athleticism, and skill makes her a matchup nightmare for opponents.
