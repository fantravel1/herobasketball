üìñ Biography

Caitlin Clark is a generational talent who has transformed women's basketball. The Indiana Fever point guard entered the WNBA as the most hyped prospect in league history, carrying the momentum of an unprecedented college career at the University of Iowa.

Born on January 22, 2002, in Des Moines, Iowa, Clark became the NCAA Division I all-time leading scorer (men's or women's) with 3,951 career points. Her deep three-pointers, flashy passes, and fierce competitiveness made her a cultural phenomenon, drawing record crowds and TV ratings throughout her college career.

Selected first overall in the 2024 WNBA Draft by the Indiana Fever, Clark immediately delivered on the hype, winning Rookie of the Year and leading the league in assists while helping revitalize the Fever franchise and driving unprecedented interest in women's basketball.
