üìñ Biography

Paige Bueckers is one of the most electrifying guards in women's basketball and the projected #1 pick in the 2025 WNBA Draft. The UConn Huskies star made history as the first freshman to win AP Player of the Year in 2021, leading the Huskies to the Final Four with her exceptional playmaking and scoring ability.

Born on October 20, 2001, in Edina, Minnesota, Bueckers arrived at UConn as the most heralded recruit in program history. Despite suffering multiple knee injuries including ACL surgery, she has demonstrated remarkable resilience, returning stronger each time. Her court vision, shooting touch, and basketball IQ have drawn comparisons to WNBA legends.

In her senior season (2024-25), Bueckers is on a mission to deliver a national championship to UConn while showcasing why she's considered a generational talent. Her leadership, clutch performances, and ability to elevate teammates make her one of the most complete players in college basketball history.
