Ariel Atkins is one of the WNBA's premier two-way guards. The 2018 first-round pick has developed into a cornerstone for the Washington Mystics, helping them win the 2019 WNBA championship in her second season.

A native of Dallas, Atkins played college basketball at Texas, where she was a two-time All-Big 12 selection. Her combination of shooting, playmaking, and elite perimeter defense has made her a two-time WNBA All-Star and earned her a spot on the 2020 Olympic gold medal-winning Team USA roster.

Atkins is known for her leadership, competitive fire, and ability to perform in clutch moments. She has emerged as one of the faces of the Mystics franchise and a vocal leader both on and off the court.
