Sophie Cunningham has become one of the most popular players in the WNBA thanks to her fearless, physical style of play and lights-out three-point shooting. The Phoenix Mercury guard/forward has carved out a role as a key contributor and fan favorite.

Cunningham played college basketball at Missouri, where she became the program's all-time leading scorer and a two-time SEC Player of the Year. She was drafted by Phoenix in the 2019 WNBA Draft and has developed into a reliable rotation player known for her toughness and shooting.

Known for her competitive edge and never-back-down attitude, Cunningham has built a passionate following. Her three-point shooting and willingness to take on any opponent have made her an important piece of the Mercury's rotation alongside Diana Taurasi and Brittney Griner.
