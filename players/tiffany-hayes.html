Tiffany Hayes

Las Vegas AcesGuardUSA5'10"
WNBA Champion 2023 3x All-Star UConn

Tiffany Hayes came out of retirement to join the Las Vegas Aces and immediately contributed to their 2023 championship run. Her explosive scoring ability and clutch performances have defined her career.

A UConn champion and three-time All-Star, Hayes brings veteran savvy and instant offense off the bench. Her ability to create her own shot and score in bunches makes her a valuable weapon.
