Candice Dupree

WNBA Legend (Retired)ForwardUSA6'2"
7x All-Star Temple Legend Top 10 Scorer

Candice Dupree retired as one of the greatest scorers in WNBA history. A seven-time All-Star, she was a consistent force for over 16 seasons in the league.

Known for her smooth mid-range game and post moves, Dupree accumulated over 6,000 career points. Her longevity and consistency make her one of the all-time greats.
