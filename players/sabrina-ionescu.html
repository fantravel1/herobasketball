üìñ Biography

Sabrina Ionescu is one of the most complete players in women's basketball, known for her incredible court vision, clutch shooting, and historic triple-double performances. The New York Liberty guard has transcended the sport with iconic moments like winning the NBA All-Star 3-Point Contest.

Born on December 6, 1997, in Walnut Creek, California, Ionescu became a legend at the University of Oregon. She holds the NCAA record for career triple-doubles (26) and is the only player in NCAA history (men's or women's) to record 2,000 points, 1,000 rebounds, and 1,000 assists.

Selected first overall by the New York Liberty in 2020, Ionescu has transformed the franchise alongside Breanna Stewart, leading them to consecutive Finals appearances and establishing herself as one of the game's elite players.
