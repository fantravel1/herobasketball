Penny Taylor

Phoenix Mercury (Retired)ForwardAustralia6'1"
2x WNBA Champion 3x Olympic Medalist Australian Legend

Penny Taylor is one of the greatest international players in WNBA history. A two-time champion with Phoenix, she formed a legendary partnership with Diana Taurasi that defined the Mercury franchise.

An Australian Opals icon with three Olympic medals, Taylor's versatility and basketball IQ made her invaluable. She is now married to Diana Taurasi and works in the Mercury front office.
