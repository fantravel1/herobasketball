Brittney Sykes

Washington MysticsGuardUSA5'9"
All-Defensive Team Lockdown Defender Syracuse

Brittney Sykes is one of the premier perimeter defenders in the WNBA. Known for her tenacity and ability to guard the opponent's best player, she has earned multiple All-Defensive Team selections throughout her career.

A Syracuse alum, Sykes combines elite athleticism with high basketball IQ to disrupt offenses. Her defensive prowess makes her an invaluable asset to any team looking to build a championship-caliber defense.
