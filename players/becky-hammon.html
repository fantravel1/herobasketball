Becky Hammon

Las Vegas Aces (Coach)Point GuardUSA5'6"
6x All-Star Hall of Fame 2x WNBA Champion Coach

Becky Hammon is a WNBA legend and pioneer. A six-time All-Star with the New York Liberty and San Antonio Stars, she became one of the greatest point guards in league history.

After retiring, Hammon became the first woman to serve as a full-time NBA assistant coach. Now as head coach of the Las Vegas Aces, she has won back-to-back WNBA championships (2022, 2023).
