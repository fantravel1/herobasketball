Diamond DeShields is an explosive scorer known for her athleticism and ability to get to the basket. The 2018 first-round pick won a WNBA championship with the Chicago Sky in 2019 and earned All-Star honors the same year.

DeShields played college basketball at North Carolina and Tennessee, showcasing her elite scoring ability. In the WNBA, she has played for the Sky and Phoenix Mercury, consistently providing offensive firepower and the ability to create her own shot.

Her combination of speed, athleticism, and scoring instincts make her one of the most dynamic guards in the league. DeShields continues to be a key contributor wherever she plays.
