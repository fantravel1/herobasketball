Odyssey Sims

WNBA VeteranGuardUSA5'8"
All-Star NCAA Champion Baylor

Odyssey Sims is an explosive scorer who can take over games with her ability to create offense. A Baylor legend and NCAA champion, she brought that scoring prowess to the WNBA.

Her quickness and ability to get to the rim make her a constant threat. Sims has proven throughout her career that she can be a primary scoring option at the professional level.
