Cappie Pondexter

Phoenix Mercury (Retired)GuardUSA5'9"
2x WNBA Champion 2x Finals MVP 5x All-Star

Cappie Pondexter was one of the most electrifying players in WNBA history. A two-time champion and two-time Finals MVP with Phoenix, she could take over games with her scoring prowess.

Her combination of scoring ability, playmaking, and competitive fire made her one of the premier guards of her era. Pondexter finished her career with over 5,000 points.
