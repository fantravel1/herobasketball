Dawn Staley

South Carolina (Coach)Point GuardUSA5'6"
3x Olympic Gold Hall of Fame 3x NCAA Champion Coach

Dawn Staley is one of basketball's most accomplished figures as both a player and coach. A three-time Olympic gold medalist and Hall of Famer, she was a dominant point guard during her playing career.

As head coach at South Carolina, Staley has built a dynasty, winning three NCAA championships (2017, 2022, 2024). She is also the head coach of Team USA, continuing her legacy of excellence.
