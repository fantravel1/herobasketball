What is the WCBA?

The WCBA (Women's Chinese Basketball Association) is China's top professional women's basketball league, founded in 2002. It features 17 teams and is known for developing some of the tallest players in women's basketball, including 7'3" Han Xu who plays for the New York Liberty in the WNBA.

Which WCBA players have played in the WNBA?

Several WCBA-developed players have gone on to the WNBA, including Han Xu (New York Liberty), Li Yueru, and historically Zheng Haixia. The 2024 WNBA draft saw increased interest in Chinese prospects as teams look for size and international talent.

How does the WCBA compare to other Asian leagues?

The WCBA is considered the strongest women's basketball league in Asia. China's national team has won multiple Asian Games gold medals, and the league's investment in youth development and infrastructure exceeds other Asian leagues like the WKBL (South Korea) and WJBL (Japan).
