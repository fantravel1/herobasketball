What is the WNBL?

The WNBL (Women's National Basketball League) is Australia's premier women's basketball league, founded in 1981. It features 8 teams from across Australia and has produced numerous WNBA players and Olympic medalists for the Australian Opals national team. The league is known for its physicality and strong fundamental basketball.

Which WNBL players have played in the WNBA?

Numerous WNBL alumni have gone on to successful WNBA careers, including Lauren Jackson (one of the greatest players ever), Liz Cambage, Stephanie Talbot, Ezi Magbegor (Seattle Storm), and Alanna Smith. Australia consistently produces WNBA talent, with over 20 Australian players having competed in the league.

When is the WNBL season?

The WNBL season runs from November to March, during the WNBA offseason. This timing allows WNBA players to compete in Australia during their break, and Australian players can play in the WNBA during their offseason, maximizing playing time year-round.
