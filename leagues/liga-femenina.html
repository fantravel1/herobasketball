What is Liga Femenina Endesa?

Liga Femenina Endesa is Spain's top professional women's basketball league, founded in 1964. It features 14 teams competing annually for the national championship and is one of the strongest women's basketball leagues in Europe. The league has produced numerous players who have gone on to star in the WNBA and European competitions.

Which team has won the most Liga Femenina titles?

Perfumerias Avenida Salamanca is the most successful team in modern Liga Femenina history with 6 league titles. They have also won the EuroLeague Women championship in 2011, making them one of Spain's most decorated clubs.

Do WNBA players compete in Liga Femenina?

Yes, many WNBA players compete in Liga Femenina during the WNBA offseason. Spanish clubs offer competitive salaries and the league's high level of competition makes it an attractive destination for American stars. Notable WNBA players who have played in Spain include Breanna Stewart, Tina Charles, and Sylvia Fowles.
