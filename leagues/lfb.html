What is the LFB?

The LFB (Ligue Feminine de Basketball) is France's top professional women's basketball league, founded in 1938. It is one of the oldest women's basketball leagues in the world and features 12 teams competing for the French national championship. The league has produced numerous Olympic medalists and WNBA players.

Which team has won the most LFB titles?

Bourges Basket is the most successful team in LFB history with 14 league titles. They have also won 3 EuroLeague Women championships (1997, 1998, 2001), making them one of Europe's most decorated women's basketball clubs.

What is the connection between the LFB and the WNBA?

Many WNBA players compete in the LFB during the WNBA offseason. French clubs offer competitive salaries and the league's strong infrastructure attracts American and international talent. Notable WNBA players who have played in France include Diana Taurasi and Sue Bird.
