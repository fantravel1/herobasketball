üìñ About Christie Sides

Christie Sides served as head coach of the Indiana Fever from 2023-2024, guiding the franchise through a transformative period that included Caitlin Clark's historic rookie season. Under her leadership, the Fever returned to the WNBA playoffs in 2024 after a seven-year drought.

Before joining Indiana, Sides accumulated extensive WNBA coaching experience as an assistant with the Atlanta Dream, Chicago Sky, and Indiana Fever. She played a key role in developing young talent and implementing offensive systems across multiple franchises. Her coaching career began in the collegiate ranks before transitioning to the professional level.

Sides' tenure with the Fever was highlighted by managing one of the most scrutinized rookie seasons in WNBA history with Caitlin Clark, while also developing young stars like Aliyah Boston and NaLyssa Smith. Her experience working with guards and emphasis on up-tempo offense helped modernize the Fever's playing style during a critical rebuilding phase.
