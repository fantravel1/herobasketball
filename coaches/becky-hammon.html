üìñ About Becky Hammon

Becky Hammon is one of the most successful coaches in WNBA history and a pioneer for women in professional basketball coaching. In 2022, she became the head coach of the Las Vegas Aces and immediately led them to back-to-back WNBA Championships in 2022 and 2023.

Before coaching, Hammon was a legendary WNBA player, earning six All-Star selections and finishing as one of the league's all-time leaders in points and assists with the New York Liberty and San Antonio Silver Stars. She was named one of the WNBA's 25 Greatest Players.

Hammon made history in 2014 when she became the first full-time female assistant coach in NBA history with the San Antonio Spurs under Gregg Popovich. In 2020, she became the first woman to serve as acting head coach in an NBA game. Her success with the Aces has made her the first WNBA coach to earn more than $1 million annually.
