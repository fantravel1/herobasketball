üìñ About Curt Miller

Curt Miller is the head coach of the Los Angeles Sparks and one of the most accomplished coaches in women's basketball. With over 300 career victories across college and professional basketball, Miller has established himself as an elite tactician and developer of talent.

Miller's WNBA coaching career began with the Connecticut Sun, where he served as head coach from 2016-2022. In his first season, he was named the 2017 WNBA Coach of the Year after leading the Sun to the WNBA Finals. Under his leadership, Connecticut became one of the league's most consistent playoff teams, known for their defensive prowess and disciplined execution.

Before joining the WNBA, Miller spent 17 successful years as the head coach at Bowling Green State University, amassing over 250 wins and multiple MAC championships. He joined the Los Angeles Sparks in 2023 as head coach and general manager, tasked with rebuilding one of the WNBA's most storied franchises. His extensive experience, innovative offensive schemes, and commitment to player development make him one of the league's most respected coaches.
