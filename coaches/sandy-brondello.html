üìñ About Sandy Brondello

Sandy Brondello is an Australian basketball icon who led the New York Liberty to their first WNBA Championship in franchise history in 2024. Her victory ended a 28-year title drought for New York and cemented her status as one of the premier coaches in the WNBA.

Before joining the Liberty in 2022, Brondello spent seven seasons as head coach of the Phoenix Mercury, leading them to the 2014 WNBA Finals. She has consistently built competitive teams and is known for her offensive systems and player development skills.

As a player, Brondello represented Australia at four Olympic Games, winning three bronze medals (1996, 2000, 2012). She played professionally in Australia and Europe, earning recognition as one of the greatest Australian basketball players of her era. Her international experience has made her a respected figure in global women's basketball.
