üìñ Team History

The Seattle Storm are one of the WNBA's most decorated franchises, winning four championships across three different eras. Founded in 2000, the Storm quickly became a powerhouse thanks to legendary players like Lauren Jackson and Sue Bird.

The first two titles came in 2004 and 2010 with the Jackson-Bird duo. After a rebuilding period, the Storm reloaded with Breanna Stewart (now with NY Liberty) and won back-to-back championships in 2018 and 2020.

Now led by scoring champion Jewell Loyd and coached by former Storm player Noelle Quinn, Seattle continues its tradition of excellence. The team plays at Climate Pledge Arena, one of the most innovative venues in sports.
