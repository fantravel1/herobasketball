The Indiana Fever joined the WNBA in 2000 and won their only championship in 2012, led by legendary guard Tamika Catchings. The franchise has experienced ups and downs but is now entering an exciting new era.

The 2024 season marked a turning point with the arrival of Caitlin Clark, the #1 overall pick and the most hyped prospect in WNBA history. Alongside 2023 Rookie of the Year Aliyah Boston, the Fever have built a young core that promises to compete for years to come.

Clark's presence has revitalized the franchise, driving record attendance and TV ratings while leading the Fever back to the playoffs for the first time since 2016.
