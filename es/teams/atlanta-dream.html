üìñ Team History

The Atlanta Dream joined the WNBA in 2008 and quickly became contenders, reaching the WNBA Finals three times (2010, 2011, 2013). Though still seeking their first championship, the franchise has a proud history of competitive basketball.

The early Dream teams featured stars like Angel McCoughtry, Sancho Lyttle, and Erika de Souza. McCoughtry remains the franchise's all-time leading scorer and one of the most dynamic players in WNBA history.

Under new ownership since 2021, the Dream are building for the future with young talents like Rhyne Howard (2022 Rookie of the Year) and All-Star Allisha Gray. The team is committed to bringing a championship to Atlanta.
