Jordin Canada is a dynamic floor general who has brought elite playmaking and leadership to the Los Angeles Sparks. The speedy point guard returned to her hometown team in 2023, adding veteran savvy and championship experience to a young Sparks roster.

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Canada became a UCLA legend, leading the Bruins to back-to-back Final Four appearances in 2017 and 2018. Her quickness, court vision, and ability to break down defenses made her one of the Pac-12's most electrifying players and a top-five pick in the 2018 WNBA Draft.

After starting her WNBA career in Seattle, where she won a championship in 2020 and developed into one of the league's premier assist leaders, Canada joined the Sparks in 2023. Her homecoming has been highlighted by an All-Star selection and her ability to elevate teammates with her passing, pace, and relentless competitive spirit.
