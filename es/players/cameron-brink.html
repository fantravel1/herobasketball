Biography

Cameron Brink is an elite two-way player who was selected with the #2 overall pick in the 2024 WNBA Draft by the Los Angeles Sparks. Known for her exceptional shot-blocking ability and versatile offensive game, Brink was one of the most decorated college players in recent history.

At Stanford University, Brink won the 2024 Naismith Defensive Player of the Year and was a three-time All-American. She helped lead the Cardinal to the 2021 NCAA Championship and was named the Final Four Most Outstanding Player.

Unfortunately, Brink's rookie season was cut short due to an ACL injury in June 2024. Before her injury, she was averaging impressive numbers and was considered a frontrunner for Rookie of the Year honors. Her elite defensive instincts and shot-blocking prowess made her an immediate impact player at the WNBA level.
