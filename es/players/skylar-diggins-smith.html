Skylar Diggins-Smith is one of the most decorated point guards in WNBA history and a six-time All-Star. Known for her scoring ability, leadership, and competitive fire, she has been a cornerstone player for multiple franchises throughout her career.

At Notre Dame, Diggins-Smith was a two-time consensus All-American and led the Fighting Irish to three consecutive Final Fours. She was the #3 overall pick in the 2013 WNBA Draft by the Tulsa Shock.

After stints with Tulsa/Dallas and Phoenix, Diggins-Smith signed with the Seattle Storm in 2023. She has been an advocate for mental health awareness in professional sports, sharing her own experiences with postpartum depression to help other athletes.
