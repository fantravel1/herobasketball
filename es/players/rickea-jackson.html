Rickea Jackson is an emerging star forward for the Los Angeles Sparks, bringing versatility and scoring punch to one of the WNBA's most storied franchises. The 2024 rookie arrived in Los Angeles after a stellar college career at Tennessee, where she became one of the SEC's most feared scorers.

Born in Detroit, Michigan, Jackson began her college career at Mississippi State before transferring to Tennessee for her final two seasons. At Tennessee, she flourished under head coach Kellie Harper, earning All-SEC First Team honors in 2023 and becoming one of the nation's premier forwards with her ability to score from all three levels.

Selected fourth overall by the Sparks in the 2024 WNBA Draft, Jackson has brought instant offense and toughness to Los Angeles. Her physicality, mid-range game, and improving three-point shot make her a key building block for the Sparks' future alongside the team's young core.
