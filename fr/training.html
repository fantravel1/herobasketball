Pro Training Tips

1

Practice with Purpose

Every rep should have intent. Focus on quality over quantity in your training sessions.

2

Work on Weaknesses

Spend 60% of your time on weaknesses and 40% maintaining strengths.

3

Film Yourself

Record your workouts to identify form issues and track improvement over time.

4

Rest & Recovery

Your body grows during rest. Schedule recovery days and get 8+ hours of sleep.
