Biographie

Dearica Hamby is one of the most versatile forwards in the WNBA, known for her ability to score, rebound, and facilitate. She was traded to the Los Angeles Sparks in January 2023, where she became a franchise cornerstone and the team's leading scorer.

Originally drafted by the San Antonio Stars in 2015, Hamby spent her first seven seasons with the franchise (which became the Las Vegas Aces in 2018). During her time in Las Vegas, she won two Sixth Woman of the Year awards and was part of the team's rise to championship contention.

In 2024, Hamby emerged as one of the league's premier players, averaging career highs and earning her first All-Star selection as a starter. Her leadership and scoring ability have made her the face of the Sparks franchise.
