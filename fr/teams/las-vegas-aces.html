üìñ Team History

The Las Vegas Aces have become the WNBA's premier dynasty, winning back-to-back championships in 2022 and 2023. Originally founded as the Utah Starzz in 1997, the franchise relocated to San Antonio in 2003 before moving to Las Vegas in 2018.

Under the leadership of head coach Becky Hammon and superstar A'ja Wilson, the Aces have assembled one of the most talented rosters in league history. The team plays at Michelob ULTRA Arena, which has become one of the toughest places to play in the WNBA.

The franchise's transformation in Las Vegas has been remarkable, going from a struggling team to championship contenders almost immediately after the move, culminating in consecutive titles.
