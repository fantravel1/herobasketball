üìñ Team History

The Minnesota Lynx are one of the most successful franchises in WNBA history, winning four championships in seven years during their dynasty era (2011-2017). Coached by Cheryl Reeve, who has guided the team since 2010, the Lynx set the standard for sustained excellence.

The dynasty featured all-time greats Maya Moore, Lindsay Whalen, Seimone Augustus, Rebekkah Brunson, and Sylvia Fowles. This core reached six WNBA Finals in eight years, winning four titles.

Now led by MVP candidate Napheesa Collier, the Lynx are building toward their next championship run. The franchise's culture of excellence and player development continues to attract top talent.
