Famila Schio (officially Famila Wuber Schio) is the most successful women's basketball club in Italy, winning 7 Serie A1 championships and consistently competing at the highest level of European competition.

Based in the Veneto region of northeastern Italy, the club has been a staple of Italian women's basketball for nearly three decades. Their consistent success domestically has made them regular participants in EuroLeague Women.

While still seeking their first European crown, Famila Schio's dominance in Italy and competitive performances in Europe have established them as one of the continent's premier programs.
