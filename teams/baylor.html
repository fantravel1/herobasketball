The Baylor Bears became a national powerhouse under Kim Mulkey, who led the program to 3 national championships (2005, 2012, 2019) before departing for LSU in 2021.

Mulkey transformed Baylor from an afterthought into one of the most dominant programs in women's college basketball. The 2012 team, led by Brittney Griner, went 40-0 for a perfect season - one of only two in NCAA history.

Now under head coach Nicki Collen, Baylor continues to compete at the highest level in the Big 12, maintaining its tradition of physical defense and elite post play.
