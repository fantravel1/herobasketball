UMMC Ekaterinburg holds the record for the most EuroLeague Women titles with 7 championships. The club, backed by UMMC mining company, has historically offered the highest salaries in women's basketball, attracting the world's best players.

Based in the Ural Mountains region of Russia, UMMC has featured legendary players including Diana Taurasi, Sue Bird, and Brittney Griner during various seasons. The club's financial resources and commitment to winning made it the premier destination for WNBA players during the offseason.

Due to geopolitical events beginning in 2022, UMMC has been excluded from EuroLeague competition, but their historical dominance remains unmatched in European women's basketball.
