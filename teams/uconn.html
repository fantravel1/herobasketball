The UConn Huskies are the most decorated program in women's college basketball history. Under legendary head coach Geno Auriemma, who arrived in 1985, the program has won 11 national championships and produced countless WNBA All-Stars.

The Huskies' dominance reached unprecedented heights with four consecutive national titles (2013-2016) and an incredible 111-game winning streak that remains the longest in Division I history. The program has produced icons like Diana Taurasi, Sue Bird, Maya Moore, and Breanna Stewart.

UConn's combination of elite recruiting, player development, and championship culture has made Storrs, Connecticut, the epicenter of women's basketball for over three decades.
