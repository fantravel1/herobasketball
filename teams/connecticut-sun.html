üìñ Team History

The Connecticut Sun have been one of the WNBA's most consistent franchises, making four WNBA Finals appearances. Originally the Orlando Miracle, the team relocated to Connecticut in 2003 and has built a reputation for tough, defensive-minded basketball.

Led by the versatile Alyssa Thomas, one of the league's best all-around players, and veteran scorer DeWanna Bonner, the Sun consistently compete for championships. The team plays at Mohegan Sun Arena, one of the WNBA's iconic venues.

Despite reaching the Finals in 2019, 2022, and 2024, the franchise is still seeking its first championship. With a culture of player development and defensive excellence, the Sun remain perennial contenders in the Eastern Conference.
