Sopron Basket has emerged as one of the most consistent programs in European women's basketball, regularly reaching the EuroLeague Final Four despite being from a smaller market.

Based in the historic border town of Sopron near the Austrian border, the club has punched above its weight by developing talent and making shrewd signings. Their passionate fan base creates one of the best atmospheres in European basketball.

While still seeking their first EuroLeague title, Sopron's consistent presence among Europe's elite clubs has established them as a model for smaller-market success in women's basketball.
