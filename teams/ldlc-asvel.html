LDLC ASVEL Feminin is the women's section of the famous Lyon-based ASVEL club, owned by NBA legend Tony Parker. The women's program was relaunched in 2016 with ambitious goals to become a European powerhouse.

Playing in the new state-of-the-art LDLC Arena, the club benefits from top-tier facilities and the organizational excellence of the broader ASVEL system. Their entry into EuroLeague Women signals France's growing ambitions in European basketball.

With Parker's vision and resources, ASVEL aims to bring EuroLeague success to Lyon, following the men's team's breakthrough as EuroLeague regulars.
