The LSU Tigers captured their first NCAA championship in 2023 under head coach Kim Mulkey, defeating Iowa 102-85 in one of the highest-scoring title games in history.

Mulkey arrived in Baton Rouge in 2021 and immediately transformed the program. With superstar Angel Reese leading the way as the tournament's Most Outstanding Player, LSU brought unprecedented excitement to women's basketball in Louisiana.

The championship run featured a blend of elite transfers, top recruits, and Mulkey's proven championship pedigree from her time at Baylor. LSU has quickly become one of the premier destinations in women's college basketball.
