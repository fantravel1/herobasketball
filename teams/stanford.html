The Stanford Cardinal represent one of the most storied programs in women's basketball history. Under legendary coach Tara VanDerveer, who retired in 2024 as the all-time winningest coach in women's college basketball with 1,216 victories, Stanford became a perennial powerhouse.

The Cardinal won national championships in 1990, 1992, and 2021, with the 2021 title coming during the COVID-19 pandemic in a memorable run led by Haley Jones. Stanford has been to 14 Final Fours and consistently competes for championships.

Known for combining athletic excellence with academic achievement, Stanford has produced numerous WNBA players while maintaining one of the highest graduation rates in college basketball.
