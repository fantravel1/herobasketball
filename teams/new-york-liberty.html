üìñ Team History

The New York Liberty are one of the WNBA's original eight franchises, founded in 1997. Playing in the nation's largest media market, the Liberty have always been central to growing women's basketball.

The franchise made history in the 2023 offseason by signing Breanna Stewart in free agency, forming a superteam alongside Sabrina Ionescu and Jonquel Jones. This star-studded trio led the Liberty to consecutive Finals appearances.

In 2024, after coming agonizingly close in previous years, the Liberty finally broke through to win their first-ever WNBA Championship, ending a 27-year title drought for the flagship franchise.
