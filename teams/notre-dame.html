The Notre Dame Fighting Irish have been one of the most consistent programs in women's college basketball, reaching 9 Final Fours and winning 2 national championships under legendary coach Muffet McGraw (2001, 2018).

McGraw retired in 2020 after 33 seasons, passing the torch to former player and assistant Niele Ivey. The program has maintained its tradition of excellence, combining elite basketball with academic achievement at one of the nation's most prestigious universities.

Notre Dame has produced numerous WNBA players and continues to recruit top talent to South Bend, competing at the highest level in the ACC and nationally.
