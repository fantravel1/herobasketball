The Texas Longhorns have one of the most storied traditions in women's college basketball, winning the 1986 national championship under legendary coach Jody Conradt, who compiled over 900 wins during her Hall of Fame career.

Now under head coach Vic Schaefer, the Longhorns have joined the SEC beginning in 2024, bringing their rich history and resources to the nation's toughest conference. Schaefer, who led Mississippi State to two national championship game appearances, is working to restore Texas to elite status.

Playing in the state-of-the-art Moody Center, Texas has the facilities and fan support to compete with anyone. The move to the SEC positions the program for even greater recruiting success.
