The South Carolina Gamecocks have become the dominant force in women's college basketball under head coach Dawn Staley. The program completed a perfect 38-0 season in 2024, capturing their third national championship.

Staley, a Hall of Fame player and Olympic gold medalist, took over the program in 2008 and built it into a national powerhouse. The Gamecocks won their first title in 2017, added another in 2022 with consensus national player of the year Aliyah Boston, and capped the 2024 season with an undefeated run.

Known for their suffocating defense and physical style of play, South Carolina has become the premier destination for elite recruits. The program consistently produces WNBA draft picks and has created a winning culture that rivals any program in the country.
