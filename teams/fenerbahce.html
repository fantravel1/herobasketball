Fenerbahce SK has become the dominant force in European women's basketball, winning four EuroLeague titles including three consecutive championships from 2022-2024. The club represents one of Turkey's most storied sports institutions.

Based in Istanbul, Fenerbahce has attracted top WNBA talent during the offseason, including stars like Breanna Stewart, who played a key role in their championship runs. The club's resources and professional environment make it one of the most desirable destinations in Europe.

The yellow and navy blue of Fenerbahce have become synonymous with excellence in European women's basketball, and the club continues to set the standard for professionalism and success.
