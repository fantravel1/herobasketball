üìñ Team History

The Phoenix Mercury are one of the WNBA's original eight franchises, founded in 1997. The team has won three WNBA Championships and has been home to some of the greatest players in league history.

The franchise is synonymous with Diana Taurasi, the WNBA's all-time leading scorer who has played her entire career in Phoenix since being drafted first overall in 2004. Alongside Taurasi, the Mercury have featured stars like Brittney Griner, Penny Taylor, Cappie Pondexter, and DeWanna Bonner.

The Mercury won back-to-back titles in 2007 and 2009 with their high-octane offense, then added a third championship in 2014 with Griner anchoring the paint. The team plays at Footprint Center, which they share with the Phoenix Suns.
