üìñ Club History

Bourges Basket is the most successful women's basketball club in French history and one of the most decorated in Europe. Founded in 1967, the club has dominated French basketball with 14 LFB championships.

The club's golden era came in the late 1990s and early 2000s when they won three consecutive EuroLeague Women championships (1997, 1998, 2001). This remarkable achievement established Bourges as a powerhouse in European basketball.

Known for their iconic orange colors and passionate fan base in central France, Bourges continues to be a force in French and European basketball. The club has been home to numerous French national team players and international stars.
