üìñ Club History

KB Stars, owned by KB Financial Group (KB Kookmin Bank), is one of South Korea's most successful and well-funded women's basketball programs. The team represents the corporate-owned model that characterizes the WKBL.

Based in Cheongju, the team benefits from the substantial financial backing of Korea's largest financial holding company. This support allows KB Stars to attract top talent and provide professional-grade facilities and coaching.

KB Stars has won multiple WKBL championships and has been a consistent contender for titles. The team has produced numerous Korean national team players and has attracted foreign players looking to compete in Asia's elite women's basketball leagues.
