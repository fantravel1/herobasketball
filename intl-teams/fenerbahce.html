üìñ Club History

Fenerbahce is Turkey's most successful women's basketball club and one of the dominant forces in European women's basketball. The club's yellow and navy blue colors are recognized across Europe as synonymous with excellence.

The club achieved an incredible three-peat in the EuroLeague Women competition (2022-2024), cementing their status as the premier destination for top international talent. Their roster has included WNBA stars like Breanna Stewart and Diana Taurasi during the offseason.

Playing their home games in Istanbul, Fenerbahce benefits from passionate Turkish basketball fans and significant investment from the multisport club. The team regularly competes for both domestic and European championships.
