Spar Gran Canaria represents women's basketball from Spain's Canary Islands, an archipelago in the Atlantic Ocean. The team competes in Liga Femenina, Spain's top women's professional league.

Based in Las Palmas, the club brings elite basketball to the islands and has developed a passionate fanbase. The team's yellow and blue colors reflect Gran Canaria's identity and the surrounding Atlantic waters.
