Fujitsu Red Wave is backed by Fujitsu, one of Japan's largest IT companies. Based in Kanagawa Prefecture near Tokyo, the team competes at the top tier of Japanese women's basketball.

The Red Wave have won multiple WJBL championships and contributed numerous players to the Japanese national team. Their distinctive red colors represent Fujitsu's corporate identity.
