Samsung Life Blueminx is backed by Samsung Life Insurance, part of the Samsung conglomerate - South Korea's largest business group. The team benefits from world-class corporate resources and represents Samsung's commitment to sports development.

Based in Yongin, south of Seoul, the team competes at the elite level of Korean women's basketball. Samsung's investment ensures top-tier facilities, coaching, and player development programs that rival any professional sports organization globally.

The Blueminx have produced numerous Korean national team players and have been consistent championship contenders. The team's blue colors and professional approach have made them one of the WKBL's most respected franchises.
