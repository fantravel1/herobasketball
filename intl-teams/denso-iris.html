Denso Iris is backed by Denso Corporation, a major Toyota supplier and one of the world's largest automotive parts manufacturers. Based in Aichi Prefecture, the team competes at the highest level of Japanese women's basketball.

The Iris have won multiple WJBL championships and consistently field competitive rosters featuring top Japanese talent and national team players. Their pink/magenta colors are distinctive in the league.
