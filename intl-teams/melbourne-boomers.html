üìñ Club History

The Melbourne Boomers are one of Australia's most iconic women's basketball franchises, representing Melbourne in the WNBL since 1984. The purple and gold colors have become synonymous with excellence in Australian basketball.

The Boomers have been the destination for many Australian Opals players, including legends who have represented Australia at multiple Olympic Games. The club's connection to the national team has made it a cornerstone of Australian basketball development.

With 4 WNBL championships, including recent success in 2022 and 2024, the Boomers continue to be one of the premier franchises in Australian women's basketball. Their passionate fan base at games creates one of the best atmospheres in the league.
