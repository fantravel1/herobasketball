Toyota Antelopes is backed by Toyota Motor Corporation, one of the world's largest companies. Based in Aichi Prefecture - Toyota's headquarters region - the team benefits from world-class corporate resources and infrastructure.

The Antelopes have been one of the WJBL's most successful franchises, winning multiple championships and consistently competing at the highest level. Toyota's investment in the program ensures top facilities, coaching, and player development.

The team has produced numerous Japanese national team players and has contributed to Japan's success in international competition, including the historic silver medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. The Antelopes represent the best of Japanese corporate basketball.
