Niigata Albirex BB Rabbits is part of the Albirex sports organization from Niigata Prefecture. The team competes in the WJBL, representing the Sea of Japan coast region in elite women's basketball.

Part of the broader Albirex sports group that includes professional football and basketball teams, the BB Rabbits bring top-level women's basketball to the Niigata area. Their orange colors represent the Albirex brand and the team's energetic style of play.
