ESBVA-LM Villeneuve d'Ascq is one of France's most successful women's basketball clubs. Based in the Lille metropolitan area in northern France, the team has won multiple LFB championships and regularly competes in EuroLeague Women.

The club has a rich history of developing French basketball talent and attracting top international players. Their yellow and black colors represent the team's tradition and the pride of northern France's basketball community.
