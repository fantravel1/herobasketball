üìñ Club History

Southside Flyers, originally known as the Dandenong Rangers, is one of Victoria's premier WNBL franchises. Based in Melbourne's southeastern suburbs, the club has been a fixture in Australian women's basketball for over three decades.

The club rebranded to Southside Flyers in 2019, marking a new era with fresh investment and renewed ambition. The transformation coincided with immediate on-court success, including WNBL championship victories that established the Flyers as one of Australia's top teams.

The Flyers have been instrumental in developing Australian national team players and have attracted WNBA talent during the WNBL season. Their community focus and professional approach have made them a model franchise in Australian women's basketball.
