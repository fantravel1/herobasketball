## What is HeroBasketball?

HeroBasketball is the **world's first free, comprehensive women's basketball encyclopedia** covering players, teams, leagues, training, and history in English, Spanish, and French.

We exist because women's basketball deserves the same depth of coverage that men's basketball has enjoyed for decades.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes HeroBasketball different?

HeroBasketball is the only **free, trilingual, mobile-first** resource dedicated exclusively to women's basketball. While other sites focus on NBA or require subscriptions, we provide comprehensive global coverage at no cost.

### Is HeroBasketball really free?

Yes. HeroBasketball is **100% free** with no paywalls, no premium tiers, and no registration required. Our mission is accessibility.

### How often is content updated?

Player stats and team rosters are updated **weekly during season**. Historical content and training guides are reviewed monthly for accuracy.

### What leagues does HeroBasketball cover?

We cover **40+ women's basketball leagues** including WNBA, NCAA, EuroLeague Women, Liga Femenina, LFB, WCBA, and many more across six continents.

### Can I contribute to HeroBasketball?

Yes! We welcome contributions from the women's basketball community. See our contributor guidelines for details on submitting content.

### How can I report an error?

If you find inaccurate information, please [open an issue](https://github.com/fantravel1/herobasketball/issues) on our GitHub repository.
